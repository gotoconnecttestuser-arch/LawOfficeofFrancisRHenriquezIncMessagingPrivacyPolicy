Messaging Privacy Policy (SMS Program Only)

Effective Date: 2/25/2026

The Law Office of Francis R. Henriquez, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Messaging Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you opt in to receive SMS/text messages from our office.

This policy applies only to our SMS messaging communications.

1. Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from us, we collect:

Your mobile phone number
Your name (if provided)
Details relevant to your case (if applicable)
Your SMS opt-in consent record
Message history between you and our office
We collect only the information necessary to provide direct communication related to our legal services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

Send appointment reminders

Provide case status notifications

Communicate important legal deadlines
Deliver essential case-related updates or other notifications directly related to your legal matters

3. Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect SMS-related information from unauthorized access or misuse.

4. Legal Disclosure

We do not disclose SMS-related personal data to external organizations unless required by valid court order or binding legal process, and only to the minimum extent necessary.

5. Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You will receive a confirmation of your opt-out and will no longer receive SMS messages unless you re-subscribe. For help, reply HELP.

6. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Messaging Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised Effective Date.
